A little bit about Crochet & Coffee

Hi, I'm Jayne and welcome to Crochet & Coffee.

It was my grandmother who first put a crochet hook in my hand. All these years later, there's nothing I enjoy more than passing on the skills she taught me to other crochet enthusiasts.

I first started giving crochet lessons back in 2016 and my daughter came on board a couple of years later. Whether you've never tried crochet before, or you want to take your skills to the next level, my daughter and I will tailor each session to help you develop 'crochet confident' at your own pace.

Come along and say hi! We can't wait for you to become a part of the Crochet & Coffee community.

Why join us at Crochet & Coffee?

Make Friends

Crochet & Coffee sessions are a great way to make friends over wool... everyone is welcome

Wellbeing

Relax your mind by busying your hands and creating beautiful pieces to show off to everyone

New Skills
